  • Points 15
  • Questions 8
  • Available Oct 5 at 1pm - Oct 25 at 11:59pm
  • Time Limit 5 Minutes
  • Allowed Attempts 2

Instructions

The purpose of this assignment is to encourage students to understand how the grace of God is displayed in Jesus Christ.

The quiz is due on October 11th and will be locked on October 25rd.

Take this quiz after you have read pages 121-130 in "Called to Believe," and after you watch Mr. Siegert's 2nd video on Chapter 8. Links to an external site.  

  • You have 6 minutes to take it. 
  • You can take it twice. Your highest score will be posted.
  • Answer all questions based on the book and Mr. Siegert's video.
  • You can use your brain, your notes, and your copy of the book, but you cannot use any other resources.
  • You cannot get help from another person.
  • You cannot use the internet.
  • You cannot use another person's notes or another person's book.

 

Here is a strategy that will help you get the most benefit from this quiz. 

  • Before you take this quiz, read pages 121-130 and watch Mr. Siegert's video Links to an external site..
  • Early in the week, take this quiz once. Using a pen and paper, make quick notes about the more difficult questions.
  • Review those pages, specifically looking for answers to the questions. 
  • Take the quiz a 2nd time.
